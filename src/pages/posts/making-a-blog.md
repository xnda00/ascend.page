# Making a blog
Younger I dreamt of having one but being born too late for myspace to still be cool and being too young to understand the benefits of frequently documenting my work, this dream slowly faded away as frivolous.

## But that's when ASTRO came in !

<img class="blog-img blog-left" src="/img/posts/memes/omgcat.jpg" alt="omg cat"/>

1. Specifically designed with markdown and MDX in mind
2. BLAZINGLY FAST
3. Efficient and lightweight JavaScript usage
4. Extensive integration ecosystem
5. Island Architecture to use multiple other frameworks (not that I know any other framework)

Had my past prayers been answered 15 years too late ..? No matter the answer, it relit my interest in having a blog and documenting my work !

That is how it all started.

## So, where are we headed ?

The few design principles, paper and a pen then my little figma skills all got together in a effort to bring forth a blog; The first and final idea I got was having a wave pass through my web page, maybe using shaders. Info bar on the left, posts around the middle, my logo on the top right.

<img class="blog-img unaltered" src="/img/posts/making-a-blog/mockup00.png" alt="paper mockup"/>

Based on this idea I made a figma prototype, for the dark and light version :

- The dark version : this smooth black background heavily leaning into glassmorphism with a wave
- The light version : a cartesian grid with a curve and cardboard sheets leaning into a student aesthetic

<img class="blog-img" src="/img/posts/making-a-blog/mockup01.png" alt="figma mockup"/>

I was set. And here was the first batch !
